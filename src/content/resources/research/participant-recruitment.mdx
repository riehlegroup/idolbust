## Why this resource exists

Recruitment is one of the easiest places for a research project to lose momentum.
This checklist keeps teams aligned on scope, screening, and follow-through.

## Checklist

1. Define participant criteria in plain language.
2. Choose two outreach channels that fit your audience.
3. Confirm incentive, session length, and timezone details.
4. Send reminders at 24 hours and 2 hours before sessions.
5. Track no-shows and update messaging after each round.

## When to use it

Use this before each interview cycle to reduce avoidable scheduling friction.
