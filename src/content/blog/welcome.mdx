## Welcome!

We're excited to launch **Idol or Bust**, a project dedicated to building simple,
maintainable website software for research projects that want to understand their user needs.

## Our Goals

1. **Simplicity** - Tools that are easy to understand and use
2. **Maintainability** - Code that can evolve with your project
3. **User Focus** - Everything designed around understanding users

## What to Expect

This blog will share:

- Updates on our research progress
- Insights from user studies
- Technical decisions and their rationale
- Best practices we discover along the way

## Get Involved

We welcome contributions from the research community. Check out our
[GitHub repository](https://github.com/riehlegroup/idolbust) to get started.

Stay tuned for more updates!
